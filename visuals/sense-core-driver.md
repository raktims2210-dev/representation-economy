# SENSE–CORE–DRIVER Visual Model

The SENSE–CORE–DRIVER framework explains how AI systems perceive reality, reason over it, and act within governance boundaries.

It separates AI systems into three fundamental layers:

- SENSE → representation and machine legibility
- CORE → cognition and reasoning
- DRIVER → execution, governance, and legitimacy

Together, these layers help explain why many AI systems succeed or fail.
