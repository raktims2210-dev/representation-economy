# SENSE–CORE–DRIVER Framework

## What Is SENSE–CORE–DRIVER?

SENSE–CORE–DRIVER is a framework for understanding how AI systems perceive reality, reason over it, and act within governance boundaries.

The framework separates AI systems into three layers:

- SENSE
- CORE
- DRIVER

Together, these layers explain why many AI systems succeed or fail.

---

# SENSE

SENSE is the representation layer.

It determines how reality becomes machine-legible.

SENSE includes:
- Signals
- Entities
- State representation
- Evolution over time

Without strong SENSE, AI systems reason over incomplete or distorted reality.

---

# CORE

CORE is the cognition layer.

It includes:
- reasoning
- prediction
- planning
- orchestration
- optimization

Most current AI innovation is concentrated in CORE.

---

# DRIVER

DRIVER is the execution and governance layer.

It determines:
- who authorized action
- what constraints exist
- how actions are verified
- how recourse works
- how accountability is enforced

DRIVER makes AI action legitimate and governable.

---

# Why the Framework Matters

Many organizations overinvest in CORE while underinvesting in SENSE and DRIVER.

As AI systems become more autonomous, all three layers become critical.
