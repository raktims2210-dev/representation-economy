# Scholarly Ecosystem

## Overview

The Representation Economy and SENSE–CORE–DRIVER frameworks are supported by an open scholarly ecosystem designed to promote transparency, accessibility, reproducibility, discoverability, and long-term preservation.

This page provides a consolidated view of the research, publication, archival, indexing, and community resources associated with the framework.

---

# Core Principle

The goal is not simply to publish a framework.

The goal is to make the framework:

* Discoverable
* Citable
* Discussable
* Critiquable
* Extendable
* Preservable

across academic, industry, and open-science communities.
