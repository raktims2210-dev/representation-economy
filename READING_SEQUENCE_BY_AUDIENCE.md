# Purpose

The Representation Economy and the SENSE–CORE–DRIVER framework touch multiple domains:

* AI,
* enterprise architecture,
* governance,
* institutional economics,
* digital transformation,
* autonomy,
* organizational design,
* and systems thinking.

Different audiences enter the framework from different perspectives.

This document provides curated reading paths for:

* executives,
* engineers,
* architects,
* policymakers,
* researchers,
* students,
* investors,
* and creators.

The goal is to help readers quickly find:

* the most relevant concepts,
* the right level of depth,
* and a logical sequence for understanding the framework.
