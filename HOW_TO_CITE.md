# How to Cite the Representation Economy Framework

## Overview

If you use, reference, discuss, extend, critique, teach, or build upon the Representation Economy framework or the SENSE–CORE–DRIVER architecture, please cite the original work.

Citations help establish provenance, support scholarly discussion, and enable future researchers to trace the evolution of the framework.

---

# Preferred Citation

## APA Style (7th Edition)

Singh, R. (2026). *The Representation Economy: A Framework for AI, Institutions, and Machine-Legible Reality*. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20315480

---

# APA Citation for SENSE–CORE–DRIVER

Singh, R. (2026). *SENSE–CORE–DRIVER: An Institutional Architecture for Machine-Legible Reality, Enterprise AI, and Governed Execution*. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.20368910

# Citation Guidance

Please cite:

* The Representation Economy when discussing the broader economic, institutional, governance, or representation concepts.
* SENSE–CORE–DRIVER when discussing the architectural framework itself.
* Both works when discussing enterprise AI operating models, machine-legible reality, representation infrastructure, or AI governance architectures.
