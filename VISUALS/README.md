# Visuals and Canonical Diagrams

## Representation Economy & SENSE–CORE–DRIVER

This folder contains the canonical diagrams, visual architectures, conceptual maps, and explanatory illustrations associated with:

- Representation Economy
- SENSE–CORE–DRIVER
- Institutional AI
- Machine-legible reality
- Enterprise AI governance
- Representation infrastructure
- AI legitimacy systems

These visuals are intended to support:

- research papers,

- articles,
- educational material,
- enterprise discussions,
- conference talks,
- and derivative analytical work.

# Purpose of This Folder

The goal of these visuals is to make abstract AI governance and institutional concepts easier to understand through:

- architectural diagrams,
- layered system models,
- conceptual flow maps,
- institutional interaction diagrams,
- governance structures,
- and representation-centric system illustrations.

The diagrams are designed to complement the canonical documents in the `/canonical` folder.

# Canonical Interpretation

These visuals should be interpreted through one core principle:

> AI systems do not act directly on reality.  
> They act on representations of reality.

The diagrams in this folder explore the implications of that idea across enterprise, institutional, and governance systems.

---

# Final Principle

The next generation of AI systems may not be defined only by model intelligence.

They may increasingly be defined by:

- representation quality,
- contextual continuity,
- governance integrity,
- and legitimacy-aware execution.
