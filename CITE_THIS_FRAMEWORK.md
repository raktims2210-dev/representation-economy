# Research Philosophy

The goal of this work is not merely to describe AI systems.

The larger goal is to help define the institutional architectures required for trustworthy AI-driven societies, enterprises, and economies.

---

# Final Principle

AI systems do not act directly on reality.

They act on representations of reality.

The quality, legitimacy, and governance of those representations may increasingly define the future of AI systems and institutions.
