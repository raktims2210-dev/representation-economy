# Representation Economy in Enterprise AI

## The Traditional Enterprise Model

Traditional enterprises were designed around:
- human coordination
- fragmented information
- workflow bottlenecks
- institutional silos

## What AI Changes

AI changes:
- information retrieval
- reasoning scalability
- organizational memory
- coordination cost
- execution speed

## Why Many Enterprise AI Projects Fail

Most projects focus only on CORE:
- models
- copilots
- automation

But ignore:
- SENSE
- DRIVER

## SENSE Problems in Enterprise AI

Examples:
- fragmented data
- inconsistent entities
- weak memory
- missing context
- poor organizational legibility

## DRIVER Problems in Enterprise AI

Examples:
- unclear authority
- weak governance
- lack of recourse
- uncontrolled execution
- compliance ambiguity

## The Shift Toward AI-Native Enterprises

Future enterprises may increasingly optimize for:
- machine legibility
- institutional memory
- decision scalability
- governed autonomy
