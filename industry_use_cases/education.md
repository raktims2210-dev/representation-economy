# Education and the Representation Economy

Education increasingly depends on representations of:
- learning,
- capability,
- attention,
- intelligence,
- progress,
- and human potential.

---

# SENSE in Education

Includes:
- learning analytics
- student profiles
- behavioral data
- assessment systems
- educational graphs

---

# CORE in Education

Includes:
- tutoring systems
- recommendation systems
- adaptive learning
- curriculum optimization
- evaluation systems

---

# DRIVER in Education

Includes:
- institutional governance
- accreditation
- ethics
- fairness
- recourse
- societal responsibility

---

# Key Insight

AI may personalize learning at scale, but education ultimately remains a human developmental process.

---

# Canonical Quote

"Education is not only the transfer of information. It is the evolution of human capability."
