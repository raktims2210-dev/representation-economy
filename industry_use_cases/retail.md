# Retail and the Representation Economy

Retail increasingly operates through representations of:
- customer intent,
- preferences,
- attention,
- behavior,
- trust,
- and personalization.

AI systems optimize these representations continuously.

---

# SENSE in Retail

Includes:
- customer activity
- browsing behavior
- transactions
- loyalty systems
- clickstreams
- location signals

---

# CORE in Retail

Includes:
- recommendations
- personalization
- pricing
- inventory optimization
- demand forecasting

---

# DRIVER in Retail

Includes:
- policy
- trust
- privacy
- fulfillment
- customer recourse
- platform governance

---

# Key Insight

Retail AI increasingly competes on:
- representation quality,
- personalization quality,
- and trustworthiness.

---

# Canonical Quote

"Retail AI optimizes representations of intent, not intent itself."
