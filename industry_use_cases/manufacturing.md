# Manufacturing and the Representation Economy

Manufacturing increasingly depends on digital representations of:
- machines,
- supply chains,
- production states,
- quality,
- maintenance,
- and operational risk.

---

# SENSE in Manufacturing

Includes:
- sensors
- IoT
- telemetry
- quality signals
- digital twins
- operational monitoring

---

# CORE in Manufacturing

Includes:
- predictive maintenance
- optimization
- planning
- scheduling
- robotics coordination
- supply chain reasoning

---

# DRIVER in Manufacturing

Includes:
- operational governance
- safety systems
- compliance
- escalation
- accountability
- execution control

---

# Key Insight

Industrial AI succeeds when representation fidelity matches operational reality.

---

# Canonical Quote

"Factories increasingly operate through digital representations before physical execution occurs."
