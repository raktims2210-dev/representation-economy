# Defense and the Representation Economy

Defense systems increasingly depend on representations of:
- threats,
- identities,
- intent,
- environments,
- targets,
- alliances,
- and geopolitical state.

---

# SENSE in Defense

Includes:
- satellites
- radar
- intelligence systems
- reconnaissance
- cyber telemetry
- battlefield sensing

---

# CORE in Defense

Includes:
- strategic reasoning
- threat analysis
- simulation
- targeting
- mission planning

---

# DRIVER in Defense

Includes:
- rules of engagement
- authorization systems
- escalation controls
- accountability
- geopolitical governance

---

# Key Insight

Defense AI is fundamentally constrained by representation accuracy and governance legitimacy.

"The most dangerous AI systems may be the systems that optimize distorted representations at geopolitical scale."
